JJ Fintax Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian company launched in 2019, providing a wide array of solutions in Direct Taxation, Goods & Service Tax, Accounting and allied services through a chat-based app.

JJ Tax delivers a comprehensive approach to complex tax problems. Our ambit of tax services is relevant for individuals, firms and corporates and range from tax compliance, to tax planning, accounting and payroll, wills drafting and execution.

We are a team of Chartered Accountants, MBA(s), Company Secretaries and Financial Management experts who possess profound knowledge and committed to deliver quality and expert services to match the client’s requirements. We continuously work to improve the client’s experience with JJ Tax and develop new solutions to address the issues faced by taxpayers.
